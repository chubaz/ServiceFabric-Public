# Transition from Wave 9

Wave 9 is complete and authoritative for application generation, factory execution, lifecycle persistence, candidate review, integration, and handoff. Wave 10 reads explicit records and references from that foundation; it neither re-executes the factory nor mutates its state.

Wave-8 provider events and usage remain provider-owned references. Wave-10 evidence may cite them but must not duplicate the provider runtime, event store, usage accounting, or credentials.
